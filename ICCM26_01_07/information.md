# Introduction

## English Introduction

Tianle Yang got his Bachelor's degree from the School of Mathematical Sciences at East China Normal University (ECNU) in 2025.
His research focuses on algebraic geometry, particularly in the field of algebraic dynamics. 
His undergraduate thesis, "Invariants, Degrees, and Orbits in Arithmetic Dynamical Systems", supervised by Sheng Meng, was awarded the 2025 ECNU Outstanding Undergraduate Thesis Award.
He has co-authored two preprint papers in related areas, one of which received the 2025 ICCM Undergraduate Paper Award.

# Title

## English Title
Algebraic Dynamics and Dynamical Iitaka Theory

## Chinese Title
代数动力系统与动力学 Iitaka 理论

# Abstract

## English Abstract
In this talk, I will first introduce the fundamental concepts of algebraic dynamics, followed by an overview of dynamical Iitaka theory.
I will then present our recent findings on certain special cases of the Kawaguchi–Silverman Conjecture (KSC), which serve as applications of the dynamical Iitaka theory.
This is a joint work with Sheng Meng and Long Wang.
